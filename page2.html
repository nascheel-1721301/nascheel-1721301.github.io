Georgian Cuisine


Some of my favorite Georgian foods:

  • Khachapuri
  • Khinkali
  • Pilmeni (Russian)
  • Churchkhela
  • Satsivi
  • Gozinaki

Khachapuri

Baked bread filled with cheese.

Imeruli Khachapuri is served pizza-style.

Adjaruli Khachapuri is served like pie, with a fried egg in the middle!

Khinkali

Meat-filled dumplings

Pilmeni

Similar to Khinkali but fun-sized! (And Russian in origin.)

Churchkhela

Walnuts connected on a string, hardened in fruit candy

Satsivi

Chicken served in creamy walnut & garlic sauce

Gozinaki

Crushed walnuts mixed with honey and hardened into candy
